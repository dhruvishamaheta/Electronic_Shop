**⚡ Electronic-Shop-Management-System**


Electronic shops often struggle with managing diverse product categories, tracking customer orders, and ensuring smooth billing and delivery. A centralized system is needed to streamline operations and improve customer satisfaction.


**Objective**

Provide a unified platform to manage inventory, sales, and customer interactions with clear traceability and analytics.


**Expected Outcome**

Efficient product management
Seamless order tracking
Consistent customer purchase history

**📌 Project Overview**

This is a .NET-based Electronic Shop Management System. It supports the complete workflow of electronic product sales — from inventory intake to billing and delivery — with role-based dashboards and analytics.


**👥 User Roles**

**🛠️ Admin**

Manage Products & Categories
Track Orders & Billing
Assign Delivery Staff
Generate Reports & Analytics

**🛒 Customer**

Browse & Purchase Products
Track Orders
View Purchase History

**⚙️ Features**

Product Catalog & Inventory Management
Order Placement & Billing System
Delivery Assignment & Tracking
Customer Purchase History
Dashboard Analytics
Search & Filter System
Real-time Notifications

**🎨 UI Highlights**

Responsive Bootstrap Design
Role-Based Sidebar Navigation
Interactive Product Cards
Timeline UI for Order Tracking
Smooth Animations & Hover Effects

**🛠️ Technologies Used**

.NET Framework
C#
Bootstrap 5
jQuery
SQL Server
